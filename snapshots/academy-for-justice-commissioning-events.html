Follow @academyjustice for news about events.

Our events programme includes monthly evening seminars with guest speakers, which take place in Bristol, London and Manchester.

We also hold a commissioning-themed annual conference.

All our events are free to attend and they offer the opportunity to network and share best practice with your commissioning colleagues.

You must be a member of the Academy for Justice Commissioning to attend these events. Membership is free. Read more about how to become a member.

To register for an event, please email your details to academy@noms.gsi.gov.uk.
