Members

The Independent Reconfiguration Panel (IRP) is chaired by Lord Bernard Ribeiro.

Its members are:

  • Cath Broderick
  • Fiona Campbell
  • Shera Chok, Director of Primary Care at Barts Health NHS Trust
  • Nick Coleman, Associate Medical Director at the University Hospital of North Staffordshire
  • Glenn Douglas, Chief Executive of the Maidstone and Tunbridge Wells NHS Trust
  • Shane Duffy
  • Rosemary Granger
  • Tessa Green
  • Jane Hawdon, Clinical Academic Group Director (Children’s Health) at Barts Health NHS Trust
  • Nicky Hayes
  • Brenda Howard
  • Simon Morritt, Chief Executive of Sheffield Children’s NHS Foundation Trust
  • Linn Phipps
  • Hugh Ross
  • Suzanne Shale
