Doing business with the CNC

Our contracts and procurement team prepares tenders, drafts terms and evaluates offers.

We may post tender notices through:

  • the Bluelight ProContract website
  • the Official Journal of the European Union, where required by the European Union Procurement Directive
  • Contracts Finder

There are a number of opportunities coming up as follows

  • Radio Equipment for the Site Security Control Rooms
  • Employee Assistance Programme
  • MOE Shotguns
  • Survey Software

If you’re interested in either of these procurement opportunities send an email to procurement@cnc.pnn.police.uk with the specific procurement opportunity referenced in the subject line.
